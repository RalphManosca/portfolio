01 — About

I'm Ralph, a full stack developer and automation specialist from the Philippines. Most of my work involves building web platforms people actually use: clinics, SaaS products, corporate sites and wiring up backend workflows that save teams hours every week.

Lately I've been deep into AI-powered automation: n8n agents, GPT integrations, Telegram bots. It's the kind of work where the complexity is real but the payoff is satisfying.

I take on clients remotely and prefer long-term setups where I can actually understand what's being built and why not just knock out tickets.
